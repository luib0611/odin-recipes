How to make spaghetti

Ingredients:

  • a box of spaghetti
  • a jar of marinara sauce
  • 1-2 pounds of ground beef or Italian sausage
  • two large pots, one for boiling the pasta and the other for making the sauce
  • serving utensils
  • salt
  • water

Steps to Cook:

  1. Cook meat, get water boiling
  2. Mix in sauce
  3. Cook Spaghetti
  4. Serve Spaghetti
